# Power Escalation System

Power escalation prevents every shot from becoming full-screen effects. Escalate visual power in readable stages.

## Stages

- `stage_0_human_readable`: body action, spacing, and attack/defense are clear.
- `stage_1_enhanced_physicality`: speed, weight, trails, impact, and cloth/debris response increase.
- `stage_2_weapon_or_skill_reveal`: weapon, spell, skill, or special ability becomes explicit.
- `stage_3_vfx_collision`: VFX binds to body/weapon/contact with impact feedback.
- `stage_4_avatar_or_domain`: dharma-body, domain, suppression, or large-form pressure appears.
- `stage_5_aftermath_scale`: aftermath shows scale, destruction, power relation, and next threat.

## Rules

- A normal shot may escalate only 0-1 stage.
- Stage 4/5 needs an establishing or power-reveal shot before the contact/impact shot.
- Avatar, suppression, and domain effects require scale contrast: small human, giant shadow, floor pattern, clouds, far silhouette, or foreground anchor.
- VFX must not hide the contact frame or reaction frame.
- If power escalation makes the action unreadable, split into power reveal, contact, reaction, and aftermath shots.
