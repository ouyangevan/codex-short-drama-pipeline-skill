# Combat Style Routes

Use style routes as original type language. Do not copy a specific IP, identifiable character, shot, or house style.

## `3d_guoman_cinematic`

For 3D guoman, xianxia, fantasy, and mythic action.

- Emphasize 3D depth, low angles, orbit/push/pull, parallax, telephoto pressure, and volumetric light.
- Body weight, cloth, hair, dust, debris, and energy trails need physical feedback.
- Supports avatars, domains, flying swords, giant arrays, mountain/river scale, clouds, and distant silhouettes.
- Prompt terms: spatial layout, parallax, volumetric lighting, cloth/hair follow-through, environmental debris, scale anchor.

## `2d_ink_anime_high_impact`

For original 2D ink martial fantasy and high-dynamic animation.

- Emphasize bold silhouette, ink smear, brushstroke energy, held impact frame, exaggerated pose, graphic composition.
- Supports short explosive contact, weapon sparks, ink-like magic, speed-line rhythm, and strong composition.
- Use original type language only, not a request to copy a known work.

## `live_action_blockbuster_fight`

For cinematic live-action-style action.

- Emphasize weight, reaction, coverage, editability, grounded physics, practical debris, and actor reaction.
- Use fewer abstract VFX unless the story requires them.
- Camera language may include stabilized handheld, low-angle pressure, over-shoulder contact, inserts, and reaction coverage.
- Do not write real fighting instruction; describe screen choreography and editable coverage.
