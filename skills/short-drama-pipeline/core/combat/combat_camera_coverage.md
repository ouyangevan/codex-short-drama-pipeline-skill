# Combat Camera Coverage

Do not ask one video prompt to carry an entire battle. Plan coverage like editable footage.

## Shot Types

- `establishing_combat_layout`: positions, distance, terrain, height, obstacles.
- `approach_charge`: rush, closing distance, threat path.
- `exchange_medium_shot`: readable attack/defense exchange.
- `contact_insert`: short close shot of contact/collision.
- `reaction_shot`: protagonist or opponent response.
- `environment_impact_shot`: floor, wall, dust, water, sparks, debris feedback.
- `power_reveal_wide`: avatar/domain/suppression scale.
- `aftermath_hold`: result hold for comprehension.
- `transition_hook`: direction, threat, or pose that leads into the next shot.

## Required Camera Fields

- `camera_distance`
- `lens_feel`
- `camera_motion`
- `screen_direction`
- `subject_priority`
- `cut_point`
- `continuity_from_previous`
- `continuity_to_next`

## Rules

- Fast motion may be fast, but it needs one readable contact frame.
- Do not use constant shake, full-screen motion blur, or full-screen flashes to fake impact.
- Constrained/fast routes need one spatial direction, one action chain, and one clear result.
- High-control routes may use key frames, references, and complex VFX when confirmed, but still need limited tactical objectives.
